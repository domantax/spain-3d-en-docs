# Cabezo del Horno Hike

A short but a rewarding hike from Rosa del Los Vientos
{: .fs-6 .fw-300 }

{: .note }
Hike goes from apartment to Cabezo del Horno peak and back. A nice spot to watch a sunset.

|:----------|:------------------|:----------------|
